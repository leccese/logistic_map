Logistic Map
The Logistic Map is a model for population growth which is the canonical example simple yet chaotic systems. Learn more here
xn+1 = rxn(1-xn)
Where:
xn and xn+1 are the percentages of the max population in the current and next iterations, respectively
r is the growth rate (birth rate - death rate)
